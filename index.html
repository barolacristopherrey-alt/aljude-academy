Our Story

About ALJUDE

We are a Jordanian organization dedicated to building the capacity of nonprofits and social organizations — so they can focus on what matters most: their mission.

🎯
Our Mission

To strengthen the organizational capacity of nonprofits and civil society organizations across Jordan and the Arab region — providing practical tools, training, and frameworks that translate into real impact on the ground.

🌟
Our Vision

A thriving civil society sector where every organization — regardless of size or resources — has access to the knowledge and tools it needs to deliver lasting change in the communities it serves.

Who We Are

ALJUDE For Scientific Care

Founded and based in Amman, Jordan, ALJUDE has been working at the intersection of organizational development and civil society strengthening for over a decade. We believe that behind every impactful nonprofit is a well-run organization — and that the operational and strategic foundations of an organization are just as important as its programs.

Our Capacity Building Platform is the digital expression of that belief: a free, open, and practical resource designed so that any nonprofit — from a small grassroots initiative to a well-established institution — can access the same quality of organizational knowledge that large international organizations take for granted.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🌍
Accessibility

Every resource we create is free and open. We believe knowledge barriers should never prevent a nonprofit from growing.

🔬
Practical Rigor

We combine evidence-based frameworks with tools organizations can apply immediately — no consultants required.

🤝

We see ourselves as a long-term partner to the organizations we serve — not a one-time training provider.

📈

Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with ALJUDE Academy in 5 minutes

📋
Step 1 — Take the Full Assessment
Start by clicking Take Assessment in the top nav. Answer 8 quick questions (one per capability) to reveal your organisation's strengths and gaps. This takes about 5–10 minutes.
🗺️
Step 2 — Review Your 90-Day Roadmap
After your assessment, visit Improve → 90-Day Roadmap to see a personalised action plan with phases and weekly priorities tailored to your scores.
📚
Step 3 — Explore a Capability
Click any capability (Strategy, Funding, etc.) to access its full learning kit: Workbook → Video Guide → Micro Lessons → Templates → Assessment. Complete all five to earn a badge.
🤖
Step 4 — Use the AI Advisor
The AI Advisor (top nav) is your on-demand NGO consultant. Ask about strategy, fundraising, team challenges — or use one of the 5 specialist modes for targeted guidance.
👤
Step 5 — Create an Account
Sign in or create a free account to save your progress, track completed lessons, and access your personalised My Profile dashboard with badges and saved resources.
